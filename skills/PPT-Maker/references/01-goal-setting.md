# 01 Goal Setting

Use this workflow to turn a vague PPT request into a clear goal brief.

## When to use

Use when the user is starting from a topic, vague idea, meeting need, report requirement, or business scenario.

## Required inputs

Collect only what is missing and important:

- Topic or working title.
- Audience.
- Scenario: internal report, customer proposal, class, pitch, training, review, defense, etc.
- Desired decision or action after the presentation.
- Presentation length.
- Expected page count.
- Existing materials.
- Tone and constraints.

## Process

1. Identify the real job of the deck: inform, persuade, decide, teach, sell, review, align, or defend.
2. Identify the audience's likely concerns.
3. Convert the user's request into one primary objective and two or three secondary objectives.
4. Clarify the success criteria: what should happen after the presentation.
5. Decide whether the user needs the full workflow or a shorter path.

## Output format

```markdown
## Goal Brief
- Topic:
- Audience:
- Scenario:
- Primary objective:
- Secondary objectives:
- Desired decision/action:
- Presentation length:
- Target page count:
- Existing materials:
- Constraints:
- Recommended workflow path:
```

## Handoff

Send the goal brief to `slide-structure`.
