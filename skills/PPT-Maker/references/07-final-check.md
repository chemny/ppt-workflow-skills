# 07 Final Check

Use this workflow to check whether the PPT is ready to deliver.

## When to use

Use when the user asks to review, audit, polish, check, validate, proofread, or prepare a PPT for final delivery.

## Inputs

- PPT file or slide content.
- Goal brief and audience.
- Slide structure.
- Data sources.
- Brand or company rules.

## Check categories

1. Logic:
   - Does the story answer the goal?
   - Are there jumps, repetitions, or weak conclusions?
2. Content:
   - Are titles clear?
   - Is each slide focused?
   - Is wording concise?
3. Data:
   - Are numbers sourced?
   - Are units and time periods consistent?
   - Do charts distort the data?
4. Visual:
   - Is layout consistent?
   - Is text readable?
   - Are pages too dense?
5. Compliance:
   - Are citations traceable?
   - Are images safe to use?
   - Does the deck follow company norms?
6. Delivery:
   - Are speaker notes or Q&A needed?
   - Are risky slides identified?

## Output format

```markdown
## Final Check Report
| Priority | Slide | Issue | Why it matters | Fix |
|---|---:|---|---|---|

## Delivery Readiness
- Status: Ready / Needs fixes / Not ready
- Main risks:
- Recommended next step:
```

## Handoff

If content or design issues remain, route back to the relevant workflow. If ready, offer `presentation-practice`.
